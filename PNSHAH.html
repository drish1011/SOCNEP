King Prithvi Narayan Shah is known to be the first ruler of the Kingdom of Nepal. Moreover, the founder of the 'Unification Campaign' of Nepal.
Born to Queen Kaushalyavati Devi and King Nara Bhupal Shah of the Gorkha Kingdom on the 7th of January 1723 (27th Poush 1779 BS) was the first child of the King.
